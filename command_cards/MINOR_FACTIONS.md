## Southern Geshurites

**Source:** 1 Samuel 27 — NOT the northern Kingdom of Geshur near Bashan.

**Roster:** Not yet defined.
**Notes:** Likely desert or semi-desert tribal people.

## Gezerites

**Source:** Some Bible translations read "Gezrites." Textual tradition is debated.

**Roster:** Not yet defined.
**Notes:** Could be treated as another small tribal group.

## Nabal's Household

**Source:** 1 Samuel 25 — David requests provisions; Nabal refuses; Abigail intervenes.

**Roster:** Not yet defined.
**Notes:** Could appear in diplomacy scenarios. Abigail eventually becomes David's wife.

**Roster:** Not yet defined.

## Joab, Amasa, Asahel

**Source:** Post-MVP expansion — David's military commanders.

**Roster:** Not yet defined (late campaign).
