# Philistines — Formation Cards

> **Faction:** Philistines
> **Philosophy:** Dominant regional power with heavy infantry, elite champions, and devastating chariots.

---

## Faction-Wide Formations

These formations apply to any Philistine unit on the Command Board.

--------------------------------------------------

### Formation: PHILISTINE DOMINANCE

**Activations:** 3

All Philistine units gain +1 Attack and +1 Defense this turn.

**Purpose:**
Broad army-wide buff reflecting superior equipment and training.

---

### Formation: IRON SHIELD WALL

**Activations:** 2

All Philistine Heavy Infantry and Chariots gain +3 Defense this turn.

They cannot be pushed or moved by enemy effects.

**Purpose:**
Anchor the front line with immovable heaviness.

---

### Formation: CHARIOT CHARGE LINE

**Activations:** 2

Choose a direction. All Chariots in the army may move 3 spaces in that direction and attack.

Each Chariot that attacks deals +1 damage for each adjacent Chariot.

**Purpose:**
Devastating shock assault with stacking damage.

---

## Unit-Specific Formations

### Achish: LORD'S DECREE / DIVINE RIGHT

**TOP ACTION — Lord's Decree**
Activations: 3

Issue a command to all Philistine units within 3 spaces.
Those units may activate immediately this turn.

**Purpose:**
Broad command authority that keeps the army moving.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Divine Right**
Activations: 2

All Philistine units within 3 spaces of Achish gain +2 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Commander aura that makes nearby units harder to kill.

---

### Spearman (Philistine): LINE OF IRON / PUSH BACK

**TOP ACTION — Line of Iron**
Activations: 3

All Philistine Spearmen within 2 spaces may attack.

Each Spearman that hits deals +1 Damage for each adjacent friendly Spearman.

**Purpose:**
Coordinated melee line with stacking damage.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Push Back**
Activations: 2

Choose an enemy unit adjacent to a Philistine Spearman.
The enemy is pushed 1 space away.
Deal 1 damage to that unit.

**Purpose:**
Defensive push that controls enemy positioning.

---

### Heavy Infantry: IRON CLAD / COLOSSAL STAND

**TOP ACTION — Iron Clad**
Activations: 3

Heavy Infantry gains +3 Defense this turn.

May move 1 space after defending.

**Purpose:**
Mobile fortress that can reposition while being nearly invulnerable.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Colossal Stand**
Activations: 2

Heavy Infantry gains +4 Defense this turn.

Cannot attack this turn.

**Purpose:**
Absolute defensive anchor for the front line.

---

### Archer (Philistine): VOLLEY OF STEEL / COVERED POSITION

**TOP ACTION — Volley of Steel**
Activations: 3

All Philistine Archers within range attack the same enemy.

Gain +2 total Attack (not +1 per archer).

**Purpose:**
Coordinated ranged destruction of a single target.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Covered Position**
Activations: 2

Philistine Archers gain +3 Defense this turn.

The first enemy that moves within 2 spaces of an Archer suffers 2 damage.

**Purpose:**
Reactive ranged defense that punishes melee advances.

---

### Chariot: WAR CHARGE / WHEEL OF WAR

**TOP ACTION — War Charge**
Activations: 3

Chariot may move up to 4 spaces and attack.

Gain +2 Attack for this attack.

If this attack defeats an enemy, may move 2 additional spaces.

**Purpose:**
Devastating shock assault with pursuit capability.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Wheel of War**
Activations: 2

Chariot may move 3 spaces.

All enemy units within 1 space of the Chariot's path suffer 1 damage.

**Purpose:**
Mobile area denial that damages everything in its path.

---

### Champion: DUEL CHALLENGE / CHAMPION'S STAND

**TOP ACTION — Duel Challenge**
Activations: 3

Choose 1 enemy unit with the highest Attack value.
Champion attacks with +3 Attack against that unit.

If this attack defeats the target,
the Champion may immediately attack the next highest Attack target.

**Purpose:**
Elite duelist that dismantles the enemy's strongest units.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Champion's Stand**
Activations: 2

Champion gains +5 Defense this turn.

Only other Champion or Hero units can target the Champion.

**Purpose:**
Defensive stance that is only vulnerable to the most elite threats.
