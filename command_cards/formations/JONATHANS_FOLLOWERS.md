# Jonathan's Followers — Formation Cards

> **Faction:** Jonathan's Followers
> **Philosophy:** Small, fanatically loyal allied force. Light but deadly in tight formations.

---

## Faction-Wide Formations

These formations apply to any Jonathan's Followers unit on the Command Board.

--------------------------------------------------

### Formation: LOYAL BOND

**Activations:** 3

Choose 1 Jonathan's Followers unit.
That unit and all adjacent Jonathan's Followers units gain +1 Attack and +1 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Tight-knit formation that makes the small army punch above its weight.

---

### Formation: SWIFT RETREAT

**Activations:** 2

All Jonathan's Followers units may move 2 spaces each.

They cannot be targeted by melee until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Disengage and reposition — no unit is left behind.

---

### Formation: FAITHFUL STAND

**Activations:** 2

When any Jonathan's Followers unit would take damage,
adjacent Jonathan's Followers units may each absorb half the damage (round up).

**Purpose:**
Shared suffering makes the small force resilient.

---

## Unit-Specific Formations

### Loyal Guard: LOYAL CHARGE / GUARD WALL

**TOP ACTION — Loyal Charge**
Activations: 3

Loyal Guard may move 2 spaces and attack.

If the attack defeats an enemy, gain +1 Attack on the next attack this turn.

**Purpose:**
Momentum-based offensive that chains kills.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Guard Wall**
Activations: 2

Loyal Guard gains +2 Defense this turn.

Adjacent allied units (including non-Jonathan's Followers) gain +1 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Defensive anchor that protects the whole formation.

---

### Elite Archer: SNIPER'S VOLLEY / COVERED RETREAT

**TOP ACTION — Sniper's Volley**
Activations: 3

Choose the enemy unit with the highest Attack value.
Elite Archers attack that unit with +2 Attack.

**Purpose:**
Precision elimination of the most dangerous enemy.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Covered Retreat**
Activations: 2

Elite Archers gain +2 Defense this turn.

May move 1 space after defending without provoking opportunity attacks.

**Purpose:**
Survive and reposition away from melee threats.

---

### Scout: AMBUSH / RECONNAISSANCE

**TOP ACTION — Ambush**
Activations: 2

When an enemy enters line of sight,
the Scout attacks immediately before normal initiative order.

Deal 2 damage instead of 1.

**Purpose:**
Punish careless enemy movement with devastating ambush strikes.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Reconnaissance**
Activations: 3

Choose an enemy unit. Reveal: Movement, Attack, HP.

All Jonathan's Followers units gain +1 Attack against that unit this turn.

**Purpose:**
Share intelligence with the whole force for a coordinated advantage.
