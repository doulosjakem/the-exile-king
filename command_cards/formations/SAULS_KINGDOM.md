# Saul's Kingdom — Formation Cards

> **Faction:** Saul's Kingdom
> **Philosophy:** Professional, disciplined army built around command authority and heavy infantry.

---

## Faction-Wide Formations

These formations apply to any Saul's Kingdom unit on the Command Board.

--------------------------------------------------

### Formation: KING'S DIVISION

**Activations:** 3

All Saul's Kingdom units within 2 spaces of Abner gain +1 Defense this turn.

**Purpose:**
Centralized command protects the core of the army.

---

### Formation: RELENTLESS ADVANCE

**Activations:** 2

All Saul's Kingdom Infantry units within 3 spaces of Abner may move 1 space and attack.

**Purpose:**
Coordinated push that leverages discipline and heavy armor.

---

### Formation: BATTLE LINE

**Activations:** 2

All Saul's Kingdom units on the front line gain +2 Defense this turn.

Adjacent friendly units gain +1 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Lock the front line into an immovable barrier.

---

## Unit-Specific Formations

### Royal Guard: ANCIENT SHIELD / IRON WALL

**TOP ACTION — Ancient Shield**
Activations: 3

When Royal Guard is attacked by melee, gain +3 Defense.

After the attack, the attacker loses 1 Activation next turn.

**Purpose:**
Immune frontline that punishes melee attackers.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Iron Wall**
Activations: 2

Royal Guard may form a wall with another adjacent Royal Guard.
Both gain +2 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Double defense through mutual support.

---

### Benjamite Spearman: TRIBE CHARGE / HOLD RANKS

**TOP ACTION — Tribe Charge**
Activations: 3

Benjamite Spearmen may move 2 spaces and attack.

If the target is an Infantry unit, gain +2 Attack instead of +1.

**Purpose:**
Anti-infantry specialization using tribal fury.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Hold Ranks**
Activations: 2

Benjamite Spearmen gain +2 Defense.

Adjacent friendly Infantry units also gain +1 Defense.

**Purpose:**
Elite defensive formation that anchors the line alongside Royal Guards.

---

### Israelite Archer: RANGED BARRAGE / SKIRMISH LINE

**TOP ACTION — Ranged Barrage**
Activations: 3

Choose a unit within range. All Israelite Archers attack that unit.

Gain +1 total Attack for each Archer in range.

**Purpose:**
Concentrated volley that can eliminate weak units.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Skirmish Line**
Activations: 2

Israelite Archers may move 1 space each.

For the rest of this turn, they cannot be targeted by melee.

**Purpose:**
Reposition archers to safer ground.

---

### Officer: BUFF / REORGANIZE

**TOP ACTION — Buff**
Activations: 3

Choose 1 adjacent allied unit.
That unit gains +2 Attack and +1 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Direct enhancement of a key combat unit.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Reorganize**
Activations: 2

Choose 1 adjacent allied unit.
That unit may activate immediately (if not already activated this turn).

**Purpose:**
Extend the activation chain through officer leadership.

---

### Elite Bodyguard: PROTECT / INTERCEPT

**TOP ACTION — Protect**
Activations: 2

Move adjacent to Abner (or the target Commander).
Until your next turn, attacks targeting the Commander are redirected to the Elite Bodyguard.

**Purpose:**
Active shield for the army's commander.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Intercept**
Activations: 3

When an adjacent enemy attacks any Saul's Kingdom unit, the Elite Bodyguard may instead take that damage.

**Purpose:**
Sacrificial defense that absorbs hits for the army.
