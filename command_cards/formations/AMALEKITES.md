# Amalekites — Formation Cards

> **Faction:** Amalekites
> **Philosophy:** Fast-moving desert raiders. Hit hard and fast, then vanish before the enemy can respond.

---

## Faction-Wide Formations

These formations apply to any Amalekite unit on the Command Board.

--------------------------------------------------

### Formation: DESERT RAID

**Activations:** 3

Choose 1 Amalekite unit. That unit may move up to 4 spaces and attack.

Does not trigger opportunity attacks this turn.

**Purpose:**
Strike anywhere on the board and disengage immediately.

---

### Formation: STEPPES FLANK

**Activations:** 2

All Amalekite units gain +1 Move this turn.

They ignore terrain penalties this turn.

**Purpose:**
Superior mobility that outmaneuvers slower armies.

---

### FORMATION: HARASSMENT CAMPAIGN

**Activations:** 2

Choose one enemy unit within range of any Amalekite unit.
All Amalekite units that can reach that target attack it.

Each Amalekite attack deals +1 Damage against the chosen target.

**Purpose:**
Concentrated harassment that chips away at a single target.

---

## Unit-Specific Formations

### Chieftain: RAID COMMAND / DESERT AMBUSH

**TOP ACTION — Raid Command**
Activations: 3

Chieftain may move 3 spaces and attack.

If the attack defeats an enemy, the Chieftain may move 2 more spaces.

**Purpose:**
Commander leads from the front, striking hard and repositioning.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Desert Ambush**
Activations: 2

Choose an enemy unit within 3 spaces.
Deal 1 damage and push the target 2 spaces.

**Purpose:**
Disruption from range that breaks enemy formations.

---

### Raider: RAID CHARGE / SCATTER

**TOP ACTION — Raid Charge**
Activations: 3

Raiders may move 3 spaces and attack.

Gain +1 Attack if this attack targets a unit with lower HP than the Raider.

**Purpose:**
Pick off weakened enemies with speed and ferocity.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Scatter**
Activations: 2

All Raiders may move 3 spaces.

They cannot be targeted by melee until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Mass dispersion that makes the army impossible to pin down.

---

### Slinger: DESERT STORM / STONE GALLOP

**TOP ACTION — Desert Storm**
Activations: 3

All Amalekite Slingers attack the same enemy.

Gain +1 total Attack for each Slinger in range.

**Purpose:**
Concentrated ranged fire that stacks with numbers.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Stone Galop**
Activations: 2

Slingers may move 2 spaces and attack once.

Movement does not trigger opportunity attacks.

**Purpose:**
Mobile ranged harassment that avoids melee traps.

---

### Desert Scout: DESERT FLANK / EYES ON THE TARGET

**TOP ACTION — Desert Flank**
Activations: 2

When an enemy enters line of sight of a Desert Scout,
the Scout attacks immediately before normal initiative order.

Deal 1 additional damage.

**Purpose:**
Ambush and punish careless enemy movement.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Eyes on the Target**
Activations: 3

Choose an enemy unit. Reveal its stats: Movement, Attack, HP.

All Desert Scouts gain +1 Attack against that unit this turn.

**Purpose:**
Intelligence gathering that grants a combat bonus.

---

### Camel Rider: CHARGE OF THE CAMEL / DESERT WIND

**TOP ACTION — Charge of the Camel**
Activations: 3

Camel Rider may move 4 spaces and attack.

Gain +2 Attack for this attack.

Ignores terrain penalties during this move.

**Purpose:**
Devastating deep strike that ignores the battlefield.

---

**BOTTOM ACTION — Desert Wind**
Activations: 2

Camel Rider may move 3 spaces.

All enemy units within 1 space of the Camel Rider's path suffer 1 damage.

**Purpose:**
Mobile area denial that cuts through enemy lines.
