# Jonathan's Followers — Command Cards

> **Faction Philosophy:** Small temporary allied force. Scenario-specific.
> Light but fanatically loyal.

---

## Faction Overview

Jonathan's Men — a small allied force that supports David in specific scenarios. Not available in all scenarios.

Available in **MVP** (scenario allies only).

## Faction Rules

- Jonathan's Followers use the same Command Card deck system as David's Company (they are allied).
- They share David's activation economy — one turn per player.
- Jonathan's Followers are scenario-locked: they are not available in all scenarios.
- Card removal works the same as David's Company (remove matching card when last unit of a type is eliminated).

- **Faction-wide formation cards:** See [formations/JONATHANS_FOLLOWERS.md](formations/JONATHANS_FOLLOWERS.md)

---

## Formation Card Rules (Faction-Wide)

Same as David's Company formation card rules. See [Formation Card Rules](COMMAND_CARDS.md#formation-card-rules).

---

## Notes

- Jonathan's Followers are a lightweight allied force — typically 3 units strong.
- Loyal Guards are the melee backbone; Elite Archers provide ranged support.
- These units are designed for short-scenario play, not deep campaign longevity.
- In post-MVP, Jonathan's Followers may become a more permanent faction with expanded roster.
