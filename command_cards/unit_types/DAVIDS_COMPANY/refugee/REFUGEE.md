# Refugee — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Support (Non-combatant)
> **Faction:** David's Company
> **Availability:** MVP

---

## Formation Card: AID / EVACUATE

### TOP ACTION — Aid

**Activations:** 3

Heal 1 HP on up to 2 friendly units.

**Purpose:**
Keep key units alive in prolonged engagements.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Evacuate

**Activations:** 2

Move up to 2 Refugees to safety (away from enemies).

Refugees cannot be targeted by melee while moving.

**Purpose:**
Protect non-combatants and free up board space.

---

## Notes

- Refugees do not fight. Their value is keeping survivors alive and drawing enemy attention away from combat units.
- Aid is the only healing option in David's Company deck.
- Evacuate is critical for protecting Refugees from melee enemies.
- When the last Refugee is eliminated, Refugee Aid is removed from the deck.
