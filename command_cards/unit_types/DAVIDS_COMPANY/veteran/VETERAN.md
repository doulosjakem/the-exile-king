# Veteran — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Elite (Upgraded base unit)
> **Faction:** David's Company
> **Availability:** Post-MVP (campaign)

---

## Formation Card: VETERAN FORMATION / BATTLE Hardened

### TOP ACTION — Veteran Assault

**Activations:** 3

Veterans attack with +1 Attack.

If this attack defeats an enemy,
Veterans may immediately attack an adjacent enemy.

**Purpose:**
Elite frontline fighters that chain kills.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Battle Hardened

**Activations:** 2

Veterans gain +2 Defense this turn.

Adjacent allied units gain +1 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Anchors the defensive line with elite resilience.

## Notes

- Veterans are upgraded base units with better stats than their standard counterparts.
- Veteran Assault is the offensive powerhouse — chain kills can end fights quickly.
- Battle Hardened makes Veterans near-immovable front-line anchors.
- High HP and heavy armor make them expensive but reliable.
