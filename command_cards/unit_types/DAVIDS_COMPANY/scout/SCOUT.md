# Scout — Formation Cards

> **Unit Type:** Light (Fast, hit-and-run)
> **Faction:** David's Company
> **Availability:** MVP

---

## Formation: AMBUSH POSITION / RECONNAISSANCE

### TOP ACTION — Ambush Position Formation

**Activations:** 2

When an enemy enters line of sight:

- Immediately attack before normal initiative order.

**Purpose:**
Punish careless movement.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Reconnaissance Formation

**Activations:** 3

Choose an enemy unit.

Reveal:
- Movement
- Attack
- Special Ability

Scouts gain +1 Attack against that unit.

**Purpose:**
Information advantage.

---

## Notes

- Scouts are the fast unit with the lowest HP and discipline.
- Ambush Position is a tempo tool — it lets you act before the enemy's initiative, punishing them for moving into your sight.
- Reconnaissance provides intel and a combat bonus, making Scouts dangerous when they know the enemy's capabilities.
- Scouting is critical for revealing enemy positions before committing your main force.
