# Royal Guard — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Heavy Infantry
> **Faction:** Saul's Kingdom
> **Availability:** MVP (enemy/scenario)

---

## Formation Card: IRON SHIELD PHALANX / IRON CLAD

### TOP ACTION — Iron Shield Phalanx

**Activations:** 3

When attacked by melee:

- Gain +3 Defense this turn (heavy iron armor absorbs blows).
- The attacker suffers 1 retaliation damage.

**Purpose:**
Armored frontline that punishes melee attackers and punishes them for trying.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Iron Clad

**Activations:** 2

Royal Guard may move 1 space after attacking.

Gain +1 Attack for that attack.

**Purpose:**
Controlled offensive push with heavy armor.

## Notes

- Royal Guard is the heaviest infantry unit in Saul's Kingdom.
- Shield Wall is the premier defensive formation — makes the front line nearly impenetrable.
- Iron Clad adds limited mobility to what is normally a slow unit.
- High discipline means they are reliable but inflexible — they cannot be kited.
