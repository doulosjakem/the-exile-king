# Loyal Guard — Formation Cards

> **Unit Type:** Infantry
> **Faction:** Jonathan's Followers
> **Availability:** MVP (scenario allies)

---

## Formation: LOYAL CHARGE / GUARD FORMATION

### TOP ACTION — Loyal Charge

**Activations:** 3

Loyal Guard may move 1 space and attack.
If the attack defeats an enemy, gain +1 Attack on the next attack this turn.

**Purpose:**
Fanatical frontline fighters that gain momentum.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Guard Formation

**Activations:** 2

Gain +2 Defense this turn.
Adjacent allied units gain +1 Defense until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Protective defensive cluster.

## Notes

- Loyal Guards are the melee backbone of Jonathan's Followers.
- Loyal Charge has a momentum mechanic — defeating an enemy makes the next hit stronger.
- Guard Formation creates a protective cluster around Jonathan's forces.
