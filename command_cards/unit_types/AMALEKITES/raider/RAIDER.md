# Raider — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Infantry
> **Faction:** Amalekites
> **Availability:** MVP (enemy)

---

## Formation: RAID CHARGE / FALL BACK

### TOP ACTION — Raid Charge

**Activations:** 3

Raiders may move 2 spaces and attack.

Gain +1 Attack for this attack.

**Purpose:**
Fast hit-and-run offensive strike.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Fall Back

**Activations:** 2

Raiders may move 3 spaces.

They cannot be targeted by melee until your next turn.

**Purpose:**
Disengage and reposition.

---

| Stat | Value |

| Melee Combat | 1d6 |

## Notes

- Raiders are the core melee unit of the Amalekites.
- Raid Charge embodies the Amalekite raiding strategy — fast, hard, then gone.
- Fall Back makes them extremely difficult to pin down.
- Low discipline (1) and HP (1) mean they are unreliable individually but dangerous in groups.
