# Chieftain — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Commander
> **Faction:** Amalekites
> **Availability:** MVP (enemy)

---

## Formation: RAID / DESERT AMBUSH

### TOP ACTION — Raid

**Activations:** 3

Chieftain may move 2 spaces and attack.

If the attack defeats an enemy,
the Chieftain may move 1 additional space.

**Purpose:**
Strike fast and disengage before retaliation.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Desert Ambush

**Activations:** 2

Choose an enemy unit within 3 spaces.
Deal 1 damage and push the target 1 space.

**Purpose:**
Disrupt enemy formations from range.

## Notes

- The Chieftain is the Amalekite commander — fast and strike-oriented.
- Raid enables the Amalekite hit-and-run playstyle.
- Desert Ambush is a disruption tool that pushes enemies out of position.
