# Desert Scout — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Light (Fast skirmisher)
> **Faction:** Amalekites
> **Availability:** MVP (enemy)

---

## Formation: DESERT FLANK / EYES IN THE SAND

### TOP ACTION — Desert Flank

**Activations:** 2

When an enemy enters line of sight:

Immediately attack before normal initiative order.

Deal 1 additional damage.

**Purpose:**
Ambush and punish careless movement.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Eyes in the Sand

**Activations:** 3

Choose an enemy unit.
Reveal: Movement, Attack, Special Ability.

Desert Scouts gain +1 Attack against that unit.

**Purpose:**
Intelligence advantage.

## Notes

- Desert Scouts mirror David's Scout role for the Amalekites.
- Desert Flank is an ambush tool — attacks before initiative.
- Eyes in the Sand is the reconnaissance card — reveals enemy capabilities.
- Amalekite Scouts have the same high mobility (4 spaces) as David's Scouts.
