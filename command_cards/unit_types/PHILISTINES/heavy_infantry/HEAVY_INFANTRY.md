# Heavy Infantry — Formation Cards

> **Unit Type:** Heavy (Slow, hits hard)
> **Faction:** Philistines
> **Availability:** Post-MVP

---

## Formation: IRON WALL / POWER STRIKE

### TOP ACTION — Iron Wall

**Activations:** 3

Gain +2 Defense this turn.

Cannot be pushed or moved by enemy effects this turn.

**Purpose:**
Immovable defensive anchor.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Power Strike

**Activations:** 2

Heavy Infantry attacks with +2 Attack.

After the attack, may move 1 space.

**Purpose:**
Slow but devastating offensive push.

## Notes

- Heavy Infantry is the hardest-hitting melee unit in the Philistine roster.
- Iron Wall makes them nearly impossible to dislodge from the front line.
- Power Strike trades mobility for devastating damage.
- Slow (1 space move) but high HP and armor make them a defensive wall.
