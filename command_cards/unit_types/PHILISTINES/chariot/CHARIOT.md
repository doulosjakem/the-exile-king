# Chariot — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Unique (Rare, devastating)
> **Faction:** Philistines
> **Availability:** Post-MVP

---

## Formation: CHARIOT CHARGE / WHEEL FORMATION

### TOP ACTION — Chariot Charge

**Activations:** 3

Chariot may move up to 4 spaces and attack.

Gain +1 Attack for this attack.

If the attack defeats an enemy,
may move 2 additional spaces.

**Purpose:**
Shock damage and rapid battlefield traversal.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Wheel Formation

**Activations:** 2

Chariot may move 2 spaces.

All adjacent enemy units suffer 1 damage.

**Purpose:**
Crowd control and disruption via mobility.

## Notes

- Chariots are a unique unit type — fast and devastating but expensive.
- Chariot Charge is the premier offensive card — speed + power + pursuit.
- Wheel Formation is a disruption tool — damages all adjacent enemies while repositioning.
- Chariots ignore terrain penalties, making them effective across all map types.
