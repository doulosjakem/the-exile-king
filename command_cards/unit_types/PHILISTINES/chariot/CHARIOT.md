# Chariot — Command Cards

> **Unit Type:** Unique (Rare, devastating)
> **Faction:** Philistines
> **Availability:** Post-MVP

---

## Formation: WAR CHARGE / WHEEL OF WAR

### TOP ACTION — War Charge

**Activations:** 3

Chariot may move up to 4 spaces and attack.

Gain +2 Attack for this attack.

If this attack defeats an enemy, may move 2 additional spaces.

**Purpose:**
Devastating shock assault with pursuit capability.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Wheel of War

Activations: 2

Chariot may move 3 spaces.

All enemy units within 1 space of the Chariot's path suffer 1 damage.

**Purpose:**
Mobile area denial that damages everything in its path.

## Notes

- Chariots are a unique unit type — fast and devastating but expensive.
- War Charge is the premier offensive card — speed + power + pursuit.
- Wheel of War is a disruption tool — damages all adjacent enemies while repositioning.
- Chariots ignore terrain penalties, making them effective across all map types.
