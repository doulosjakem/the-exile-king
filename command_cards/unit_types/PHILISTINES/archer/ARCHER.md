# Archer (Philistine) — Formation Cards

> **Unit Type:** Ranged (Standard)
> **Faction:** Philistines
> **Availability:** Post-MVP

---

## Formation: PHILISTINE VOLLEY / SHEPHERD'S WATCH

### TOP ACTION — Philistine Volley

**Activations:** 3

All Philistine Archers within range attack.

Gain +1 Attack for each adjacent friendly Philistine Archer.

**Purpose:**
Coordinated ranged suppression.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Shepherd's Watch

**Activations:** 2

Philistine Archers gain +2 Defense this turn.

The first enemy unit that moves within range suffers 2 damage.

**Purpose:**
Reactive defense that punishes enemy advances.
