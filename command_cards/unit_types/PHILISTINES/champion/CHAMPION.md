# Champion — Formation Cards

> **Unit Type:** Elite (Duellists)
> **Faction:** Philistines
> **Availability:** Post-MVP

---

## Formation: DUELIST'S STANCE / CHAMPION'S RESPITE

### TOP ACTION — Duelist's Stance

**Activations:** 3

Choose 1 enemy unit.
Champion attacks with +2 Attack.

If this attack defeats an enemy champion or hero,
Champion may attack again immediately.

**Purpose:**
Elite duelist that targets high-value enemies.

---

### BOTTOM ACTION — Champion's Respite

**Activations:** 2

Champion gains +3 Defense this turn.

Champion cannot be targeted by non-champion or non-hero units.

Purpose:
Focus fire only on the most dangerous threats.

## Notes

- Champions are elite duelists — they shine against high-value targets.
- Duelist's Stance has a chain-kill mechanic against champions/heroes.
- Champion's Respite is the defensive tool — effectively immune to non-elite threats.
- Excellent counters to David's Mighty Men and commanders.
